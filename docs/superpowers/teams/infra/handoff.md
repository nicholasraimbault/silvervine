## Decisions log

- **2026-05-04** — cargo-dist version: latest is 0.31.0, not 0.22.1 as referenced in the spec. The spec was written before 0.31 shipped; 0.31 is API-compatible for our use, and `dist init` is the maintained command.
- **2026-05-04** — cargo-dist config location: 0.31 puts config in `Cargo.toml [workspace.metadata.dist]` rather than a separate `dist-workspace.toml`. Updated handoff and design assumptions accordingly.
- **2026-05-04** — Auto-merge implementation: pivoted from `pascalgn/automerge-action` (last release Aug 2023, no recent maintenance) to `dependabot/fetch-metadata@v2` + `gh pr merge --auto`. Same outcome (patch+minor auto-merge after CI), better current maintenance.
- **2026-05-04** — `ssldotcom-windows-sign = "prod"` was added by `dist init -y` defaults; removed it because we don't sign Windows artifacts and aren't shipping for Windows in V1.
- **2026-05-04** — zipsign signing: cargo-dist 0.31 doesn't natively produce zipsign signatures. The `self_update` crate's `signatures` feature can verify zipsign artifacts, but producing them needs a separate post-build signing step. Deferred to Phase 5 alongside the self-update implementation; documented in `Cargo.toml` and below.
- **2026-05-04** — cargo-deny advisories check: skipped in CI; rolled into cargo-audit instead. Reason: cargo-deny 0.16.4 fails to parse CVSS-4.0 advisories (RUSTSEC-2026-0066 specifically), which broke `cargo deny check advisories`. cargo-audit handles CVSS-4.0 fine. Once cargo-deny ships a fix, fold `advisories` back into the deny job.

## Open issues / things to revisit

2. **cargo-deny advisories** — once cargo-deny releases a CVSS-4.0-aware version (>= 0.17 expected), fold `advisories` back into the `deny` CI job and drop the cargo-audit job (deduplication).
